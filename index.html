Why ThodaCSS?

Because it's:

Lightweight

ThodaCSS weighs in at just under 7 KiB.

Simple

ThodaCSS has a clean, flat design. Nothing flashy.

Modular

ThodaCSS is separated into separate SCSS files. Use only what you need. Custom builds can be even smaller!

Mobile-ready

ThodaCSS is reponsive. Go on, resize this window to give it a try.

ThodaCSS is Open source

ThodaCSS's code is hosted on GitHub, released under the terms of the MIT license.
